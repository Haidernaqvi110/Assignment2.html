My Favourite Nodles Recipe

Quick, tasty, and perfect for late-night cravings!

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Maggi noodles
  • 1 cup water
  • Maggi masala (comes with packet)

Steps to cook

  1. Boil 1 cup water in a pan.
  2. Add noodles and masala.
  3. Cook for 2 minutes and serve hot.

Nutrition

Nutrient Amount
Calories 350 kcal
Protein 8g

Chef Tip

Extra Taste:
Add a pinch of black pepper.
